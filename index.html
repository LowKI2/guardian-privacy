Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Guardian of Ilzarah ("the Game") is developed by LowKI. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

The Game does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All game progress (scores, upgrades, settings) is stored locally on your device.

Google Play Games Services

The Game uses Google Play Games Services for leaderboards. When you sign in to Google Play Games, your public Google Play Games profile name and scores are shared with Google's services. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Games Services — for leaderboards and player authentication

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

Game progress is saved locally on your device using standard browser storage (localStorage). This data is included in Android's automatic backup to your Google account, allowing you to restore progress when reinstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
